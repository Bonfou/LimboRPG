    Armor

    There are three types of Armor in Limbo RPG: Light, Medium, and Heavy.

    Light Armor

    Light Armor offers 3 Keyword Slots and 0 AV.

    Medium Armor

    Medium Armor offers 2 Keyword Slots and 3 AV.

    Heavy Armor

    Heavy Armor offers 1 Keyword Slot and 6 AV.

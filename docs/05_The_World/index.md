Everything in Limbo, from the buildings to the people, is foreign. It all came from a different place. Nothing is native to the City. 

The Heart establishes a habitable bubble of space that loops back on itself. The Head establishes the major laws of Limbo, and the Hand enforces them. The Circles create and enforce their own laws. Thus is the way of life in The City.
## The Recursive Bubble
Limbo exists within a recursive bubble, a space that loops back on itself as though it were on the inner plane of a sphere, though there's no visible curvature. If you walked all the way in one direction from the Spine, you'd eventually find yourself arriving on the opposite side of it. Thus, there's no leaving. Those inside are trapped for all eternity.
## Technology
Technology in Limbo is a hodgepodge. From CRT displays to holograms, from sodium lamps to neon lights, technology is a ramshackle mess of a system. The "internet," if one could call it that, is a loose connection of wired networks that span the whole City.

# Life in Limbo
## The Grind
For most people, life in Limbo is an endless, mundane grind for a local Circle. Violence is common, but if you keep your head down and eyes forward, you'll likely live to see tomorrow. Unfortunately, you're not most people.
## The Inhabitants
While many disparate and unusual creatures exist within Limbo, the city is defined by its sapient population. In local parlance, "human" is a specific classification, but all sapients are referred to as **"people,"** provided they possess intelligence and free will.
### Humans
Humans are the most populous demographic in the City. They are not strictly *Homo Sapiens Sapiens*, but rather a collection of highly similar hominids capable of interbreeding.

While some may possess extra limbs, redundant organs, strange skin tones, or other anomalies, they are reliably found in all parts of the City. Most are born in Limbo rather than trapped there, having been part of the City's ecology for as long as it has existed.

However, other, stranger beings exist, such as the floating, psychic, intelligent jellyfish-like creatures known as Nidians.
### Dimension Walkers
Most people who enter Limbo are trapped permanently by the recursive bubble. However, one notable exception exists: **Dimension Walkers**.

!!! info "The Walkers"
    These are rare beings capable of circumventing the bubble and leaving Limbo. They are typically solitary figures, unaligned in major conflicts, who appear sporadically to peddle new technologies, information, or goods from the outside world.
## Ecology and Diet
Food in Limbo is defined by scarcity and the lack of sunlight. The cramped interior farms of the City mostly grow plants that thrive in the dark.
### Staples
-   **Crops:** Potatoes, mushrooms, and lichen are the foundation of the Limbo diet.
-   **Flour:** Wheat is inefficient to grow and rare. Most "flour" is supplemented with **Sump Root**, a starchy cave vine with high amylopectin content used for flatbreads and doughs.
-   **Protein:** Real meat is a luxury often imported by Dimension Walkers. The average citizen relies on insects and the eggs of the **Krezz** (a ubiquitous arachnid the size of a large rat).

!!! note "The Voidfish"
    Rarely, in contaminated streams and deep arroyos, one can find the **Voidfish**, a translucent bottom-feeder with tender, fragile flesh filled with tiny bones.
### Luxuries and Vices
-   **Water & Salt:** The Heart produces fresh water, but it has an extremely low sodium content. By reducing it massively, one can harvest **Salt**. Salt is so expensive it functions as a currency standard, backing the main currency of Limbo, the **Copper Sal**.
-   **Sugar:** Obtained by chemically breaking down potato starch (creating a cheap, bitter sweetener) or reducing Sump Root sap.
-   **Alcohol & Stimulants:** Beer and hard liquor are typically brewed from potatoes. **Cigarettes** are made from a mildly hallucinogenic mushroom that instills slight euphoria. A caffeinated algae steeped in water takes the place of tea or coffee.
### Seasonings
Spices are used to mask the taste of rot or bland starch.
-   **Bitterroot:** Powdered and used in poorer areas.
-   **Redbloom:** A sour, parasitic flowering plant that feeds on lichen.
-   **Fedder:** A type of algae that smells complex and herbaceous when dried.
-   **Oil:** Rendered from Krezz corpses or the parasitic **Oilvine**.

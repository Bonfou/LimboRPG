# Core Classes
Select a class to view its mechanical identity, core dossiers, and specialized subclasses.

??? dossier "[[Operator](03.1_Operator.md)] — The Weaponry Specialist"
    Hone your skills with weaponry to maintain pressure in a fight.
    
    - **[[Chain](03.1_Operator.md#chain)]**: Overwhelm foes with a relentless barrage of repeated attacks.
    - **[[Verdict](03.1_Operator.md#verdict)]**: Focus your resolve to strike once and strike true.

??? dossier "[[Nitro](03.2_Nitro.md)] — The High-Stakes Bruiser"
    Build overwhelming power by sacrificing your own safety and HP.
    
    - **[[Inferno](03.2_Nitro.md#inferno)]**: Burn your own vitality to grant yourself powerful combat buffs.
    - **[[Martyr](03.2_Nitro.md#martyr)]**: Sacrifice your HP to provide vital support and buffs to your party.

??? dossier "[[Lux](03.3_Lux.md)] — The Hard-Light Architect"
    Manifest and command physical constructs of solid light.
    
    - **[[Aegis](03.3_Lux.md#aegis)]**: Deploy hard-light barriers to protect your allies from harm.
    - **[[Sword](03.3_Lux.md#sword)]**: Summon crystalline weaponry to strike from unexpected angles.

??? dossier "[[Graft](03.4_Graft.md)] — The Body Alterer"
    Rewrite biological limits by altering your own form or the bodies of others.
    
    - **[[Marrow](03.4_Graft.md#marrow)]**: Distort the biology of your targets, for better or for worse.
    - **[[Chimera](03.4_Graft.md#chimera)]**: Mutate your own body to adapt to any combat situation.

??? dossier "[[Aether](03.5_Aether.md)] — The Elemental Weaver"
    Channel and control the raw elements of the multiverse.
    
    - **[[Cycle](03.5_Aether.md#cycle)]**: Specialize in a single element to master its destructive potential.
    - **[[Maelstrom](03.5_Aether.md#maelstrom)]**: Unleash unpredictable and volatile wild magic effects.

??? dossier "[[Pattern](03.6_Pattern.md)] — The Info-Broker"
    Exercise absolute control over information and neural pathways.
    
    - **[[Architect](03.6_Pattern.md#architect)]**: Analyze systems and entities to find their fundamental flaws.
    - **[[Synapse](03.6_Pattern.md#synapse)]**: Invade and manipulate the minds of your enemies.

??? dossier "[[Mantra](03.7_Mantra.md)] — The Willpower Weapon"
    Weaponize your own internal resolve and manipulate the threads of fate.
    
    - **[[Banner](03.7_Mantra.md#banner)]**: Lead from the front, inspiring allies with your sheer presence.
    - **[[Entropy](03.7_Mantra.md#entropy)]**: Distort the laws of probability to manipulate luck and odds.

??? dossier "[[Voice](03.8_Voice.md)] — The Commanding Presence"
    Use social influence and commands as your primary weapon of war.
    
    - **[[Herald](03.8_Voice.md#herald)]**: Coordinate your party with tactical buffs and battlefield commands.
    - **[[Crown](03.8_Voice.md#crown)]**: Debilitate foes through the sheer weight of your personality.

??? dossier "[[Motion](03.9_Motion.md)] — The Kinetic Master"
    Manipulate the fundamental laws of physics to control movement.
    
    - **[[Vector](03.9_Motion.md#vector)]**: Alter the speed and direction of physical objects and entities.
    - **[[Grav](03.9_Motion.md#grav)]**: Take absolute command over local gravity wells.

??? dossier "[[Fabric](03.10_Fabric.md)] — The Physical Shaper"
    Control and reshape the physical world and its chemical properties.
    
    - **[[Catalyst](03.10_Fabric.md#catalyst)]**: Brew potent potions and lethal toxins in the heat of battle.
    - **[[Tinker](03.10_Fabric.md#tinker)]**: Rapidly create and deploy mechanical devices and traps.

??? dossier "[[Engine](03.11_Engine.md)] — The Construct Controller"
    Create, hijack, and enhance artificial technology and constructs.
    
    - **[[Forge](03.11_Engine.md#forge)]**: Buff allies' gear and enhance the effectiveness of their Moves.
    - **[[Override](03.11_Engine.md#override)]**: Hijack and turn enemy technology against its owners.

??? dossier "[[Chronos](03.12_Chronos.md)] — The Time Weaver"
    Command the flow of time itself to gain a tactical edge.
    
    - **[[Oracle](03.12_Chronos.md#oracle)]**: Peer into the immediate future to anticipate enemy actions.
    - **[[Moment](03.12_Chronos.md#moment)]**: Accelerate or decelerate your own personal time stream.

??? dossier "[[Legion](03.13_Legion.md)] — The Summoner"
    Command summoned entities and biological thralls.
    
    - **[[Crucible](03.13_Legion.md#crucible)]**: Create and weaponize a unique, personal familiar.
    - **[[Anima](03.13_Legion.md#anima)]**: Bend the animals of Limbo and the Void to your will.

??? dossier "[[Heart](03.14_Heart.md)] — The Unyielding Wall"
    Endure incredible hardship and turn your own pain into power.
    
    - **[[Bastion](03.14_Heart.md#bastion)]**: Sacrifice your own safety to mitigate damage for your allies.
    - **[[Rage](03.14_Heart.md#rage)]**: Grow in physical strength as your allies suffer harm.

??? dossier "[[Wraith](03.15_Wraith.md)] — The Phantom Stalker"
    Utilize superior stealth and blinding speed to hunt your prey.
    
    - **[[Hollow](03.15_Wraith.md#hollow)]**: Vanish from sight and erase your existence from the senses of others.
    - **[[Mercury](03.15_Wraith.md#mercury)]**: Strike and move at speeds that defy the human eye.

??? dossier "[[Null](03.16_Null.md)] — The Void Channeler"
    Channel the absolute emptiness of the Void to erase existence.
    
    - **[[Stillness](03.16_Null.md#stillness)]**: Neutralize Artifice and silence the world around you.
    - **[[Eraser](03.16_Null.md#eraser)]**: Permanently remove your targets from the physical plane.

**Limbo** is a high-lethality tactical tabletop RPG set in a recursive pocket dimension suspended somewhere in the void between dimensions. It tells the stories of the billions of denizens of The City, all trapped in its looping space. Search for power, meaning, and belonging in a place where violence is an everyday language and life is as meaningless as the rest of the waste around you.

---

## The Setting: The City of Scraps

You are trapped in **The City**.
A construct known as **The Heart** generates a bubble of habitable reality within the crushing nothingness of the Void. It pulls in debris, technology, and people from infinite dimensions.

Space here is recursive. If you walk away from the city center, you will eventually arrive back at it from the other side. There is no escape.

The City is ruled by **The Head**, a mysterious council that enforces their three laws through **The Hand**. They employ a force of terrifying enforcers known as **Fingers** to crush all who would break these sacred tenants.

Below them are the **Circles**â€”syndicates, cults, and corporations that run the day-to-day chaos of the Gutters and the Rises. You work for them, or you fight them.

The rules are simple:
1. Don't interfere with the Head, Heart, or Hand.
2. Pay your taxes on time.
3. Don't use dimensional magic.

## The System: Professional Violence

Limbo splits the game into two distinct modes of play, recognizing that negotiating a hostage situation and fighting a psychic demon require different mechanical tools.

### 1. The Narrative Grid
Outside of combat, you solve problems using **The Grid**â€”a matrix of Attributes (**Mind, Body, Spirit**) and Approaches (**Force, Flow, Resistance**).

|          |    Power    |    Flow    | Resistance  |
| :------- | :---------: | :--------: | :---------: |
| **BODY** |   `Brawn`   |  `Reflex`  |   `Grit`    |
| **MIND** | `Intellect` |   `Wits`   |   `Focus`   |
| **SOUL** | `Presence`  | `Instinct` | `Willpower` |

### 2. The Combat Loop
When violence starts, the game shifts to a rigid, tactical framework.

* **Pairwise Resolution:** Every attack is a contested roll. If your Defense die beats their Attack die, you take no damage. If you win by double, you **Break** them.
* **Hardware vs. Software:** Your **Shell** (Weapon) determines your potential; your **Moves** determine your technique.
* **High Lethality:** HP is low. Damage is high.

## Who Are You?

You are not a hero. You're just someone trying to survive.

Whether you are a human scavenger or something stranger, you are defined by your ambition. You start as a grunt in the Gutters, fighting for **Cred** (XP) and **Sal** (Cash).

If you survive the politics of the Circles and the horrors of the Void, you might just become powerful enough to challenge the Head itself.

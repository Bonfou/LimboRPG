# The Economy
The economy in Limbo is split into two main resources: **Cred** (XP) and **Sal** (Money).

--- 
## Cred
Cred is the main resource you spend to improve your character. For a detailed breakdown on it, reference the [Leveling](../02_Character_Creation/02.4_Leveling) section. In short, it's split into two types: Fight Cred and Hustle Cred.
### Fight Cred
Fight Cred is spent on purchasing or leveling up **Combat Moves** (<span class="text-offensive">**Offensive Moves**</span>, <span class="text-defensive">**Defensive Moves**</span>, and **Passive Moves**). Each Level, you gain 1 Fight Cred.
### Hustle Cred
Hustle Cred is spent in one of two ways: increasing an Attribute or Approach, or purchasing or leveling up a **Utility Move**. Each Level, you gain 1 Hustle Cred.

---
## Sal
Sal is the main resource used to purchase in-game goods such as armor, weapons, food, or bribes. It's a copper coin backed by salt distributed by the Head.

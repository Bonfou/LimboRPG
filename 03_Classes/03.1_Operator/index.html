    Operator

    A knife is the most powerful form of Artifice, second only to a gun.

    An Operator excels at weapon use, forgoing fancy powers for strict adherence to tactical domination. Build Momentum with successful hits, and cash it in for big rewards.

    Core Features

    FEA Momentum

    Whenever you score at least 1 Win in a Clash or score a Break, gain 1 Momentum (max 6). Momentum doesn't decay naturally during combat, but it clears at the end of combat.

    Setup X: Moves with this Keyword allow you to spend X Momentum on them to activate the associated effect when declaring the Move. If a Move has multiple Setup costs, you must activate them separately.

    Repertoire

    Manage your 8-Move Repertoire limit carefully between Rests.

    Fight Moves

    OFF Neutralize

    DIE: 4 // PWR: 1 // DMG: 4 // EP: 2

    Setup 2: Each Win reduces the target's DIE by 1 on their next Clash, to a minimum of 1. If DIE cannot be reduced any more, reduce PWR by 1 instead.

    Rank 1

    • Increase DMG by 1.
    • Decrease EP by 1.

    Rank 2

    • Increase PWR by 1.
    • Gain Area 1.

    Rank 3

    • On 2 Wins, next Win is an automatic Break.
    • On Break: Reduce the target's DMG by half on their next Move. If the Move would deal 0 Harm, gain 1 Momentum.

    OFF Shock and Awe

    DIE: 5 // PWR: 3 // DMG: 3 // EP: 3

    Setup X: This Move gains Afflict Tremor X.

    Rank 1

    • Add Vicious.
    • Increase DMG by 1.

    Rank 2

    • Increase X by 1.
    • Decrease EP by 1.

    Rank 3

    • Add Area 1, but decrease DMG by 1.
    • If the target's DIE is reduced for this Clash, this Move gains Area 1

    DEF Weave Through

    DIE: 4 // PWR: 1 // DMG: 0 // EP: 1

    On Win: reduce the next instance of Harm taken by 1.

    Innate

    Rank 1

    • Add Deflect
    • Add Reinforced

    Rank 2

    • Increase PWR by 1.
    • Reduce the next instance of Harm taken by an additional 1.

    Rank 3

    • Add Setup 2: this Move gains "On Win: all subsequent dice gain +1 PWR."
    • Add Setup 2: this Move gains "On Loss: Reduce the PWR of the opponent's next die by 1."

    PAS Cold Start

    You begin each Fight Scene with 2 Momentum. You gain access to the Refund Action.

    Refund: this Action does not cost an Action Slot, and can be used once per turn immediately after using a Move. Expend X Momentum and gain X EP, where X is the Move's EP.

    Rank 1

    • Begin each fight scene with 3 Momentum instead.
    • Increase your maximum Momentum to 8.

    Rank 2

    • You may use Refund +1 time per turn.
    • You may use Refund on allies' Moves to grant them EP instead of you.

    Rank 3

    • Gain X+1 EP instead if X is greater than 0.
    • This Action now costs an Action Slot. Gain twice X EP instead.

    Hustle Moves

    UTL Well-Connected

    Cost: 2 FP

    You have contacts that can get items for you. Once per Chapter, you may use this Move to purchase an item less than 2,000 Sal that's illegal or regulated in your current area. It has a 25% markup if purchased this way, though this markup doesn't apply to the cost limit. This Move is unusable in the Depths.

    UTL Field Strip

    Cost: 3 FP

    You may use this Move to add or remove a Mod from a Weapon without a Check or Jury-Rig Kit.

    TRT Concealed Carry

    You may perfectly hide a one-Handed weapon of an Anomaly Tier half your own (min I) on your person. It is undetectable by standard visual inspection or pat-down.

    TRT Conditioned

    Gain two automatic Beats on all narrative Checks to withstand torture, drugs, exhaustion, or environmental effects.

    Subclasses

    Unlocking your first move in a subclass locks you into that path.

    Chain

    A barrage of gunfire erupted from the stranger's coat. When the smoke cleared, all that was left of Wallace was a mass of flesh.

    Chains excel in volume of fire and suppression, devastating targets with high Dice Moves and pressuring them with status application.

    Chain Features

    FEA Blitz

    Upon scoring at least 1 Win in a Clash, you may expend any amount of Momentum to use a single Move without spending an Action Slot immediately after the current one resolves. The Move's maximum EP cannot exceed half the Momentum spent (rounded down). You still pay the EP.

    Chain Fight Moves

    OFF Barrage

    DIE: 6 // PWR: 0 // DMG: 2 // EP: 1

    Afflict Tremor 1.

    Setup 1: On 4+ Wins, apply 6 Tremor.

    Rank 1

    • Apply Tremor on 3+ Wins instead.
    • Increase DMG by 1.

    Rank 2

    • If the target has Tremor, increase PWR by 1.
    • If the target has Tremor, increase DIE by 1.

    Rank 3

    • On Break: apply 12 Tremor instead.
    • Unopposed Dice apply twice as much Tremor.
    OFF Overwhelm

    DIE: 5 // PWR: 0 // DMG: 3 // EP: 0

    Setup 2: This Move gains "On Win: deal 1 True Harm to the target."

    Rank 1

    • Increase DIE by 1.
    • If you used Blitz to activate this Move, it gains +2 DIE.

    Rank 2

    • For each True Harm dealt to the target, apply 1 Shock.
    • If the target has a status effect and this Move scored at least 1 Win, they take an additional 3 True Harm after this Move resolves.

    Rank 3

    • Deal 2 True Harm instead.
    • This Move gains Setup X: increase DIE by 1/2 X.
    DEF Counterstrike

    DIE: 4 // PWR: 2 // DMG: 2 // EP: 2

    When rolling dice for this Move, each natural 6 rolled adds +1 DIE to the pool. Roll these dice all at once, and they cannot generate additional dice.

    Rank 1

    • Decrease EP by 1.
    • Increase DIE by 1.

    Rank 2

    • Natural 6s rolled are immune to Breaks.
    • If this Move deals at least 6 Harm, add an additional 4 Harm to its Gross Harm Pool.

    Rank 3

    • Add additional dice on 5s and 6s instead of just 6s.
    • The extra dice rolled can each add 1 additional die if they roll a natural 6.
    PAS Adrenaline

    Gain an additional Action Slot this turn if you activated Blitz last turn and spent at least 6 Momentum on it.

    Rank 1

    • Any Actions taken using this additional Action Slot cost -1 EP, to a minimum of 0.
    • If this Action Slot is used on an Action rather than a Move, refund its EP.

    Rank 2

    • Also gain 1 EP.
    • Also gain 1 Momentum.

    Rank 3

    • You only have to spend 3 Momentum on Blitz to trigger this Move.
    • Any Actions taken using this additional Action Slot gain +1 if they have at least 4 .

    Chain Hustle Moves

    UTL Breach and Clear

    Cost: 2 FP

    Activate this Move to bypass a locked door, window, or physical barrier violently. If this initiates a Fight Scene, you start with an additional Momentum. The GM may rule that a barrier is too hard to bypass, such as a blast door or brick wall.

    TRT Well-Trained

    Choose one Weapon Shell. Gain an automatic Boon on any narrative checks that involve brandishing, maintaining, or performing tricks with that Weapon Shell.

    Verdict

    "Strike sure," he said, "or don't strike at all."

    A Verdict Operator is all about precision and Power, overwhelming foes and benefitting from Breaks.

    Verdict Feature

    FEA Breaker

    Whenever you score a Break, reduce the target's AV by your Momentum on your next Clash against them.

    Verdict Fight Moves

    OFF Shatter

    DIE: 2 // PWR: 3 // DMG: 10 // EP: 2

    Setup 1: increase PWR by 1.

    Rank 1

    • Add "On Win: the target loses 1 AV for the next Clash"
    • Increase PWR by 1.

    Rank 2

    • Decrease EP by 1.
    • Add Vicious.

    Rank 3

    • Add "All Unopposed dice have double DMG"
    • On Break: the target's Armor is treated as 1 Anomaly Tier lower for the purposes of Scaling in this Clash.
    OFF Exploit

    DIE: 1 // PWR: 4 // DMG: 18 // EP: 1

    Setup 2: Add "On Break: reduce the target's next Move's PWR by 2."

    Rank 1

    • Add Add Bulwark 3.
    • Decrease Setup cost to 1.

    Rank 2

    • Decrease target's PWR by 3 instead.
    • Increase by 1, but decrease DMG by 8.

    Rank 3

    • Change "On Break" to "On Win."
    • Decrease PWR by 3 instead.
    DEF Parry

    DIE: 1 // PWR: 4 // DMG: 8 // EP: 1

    This Move causes a Break if a die is 4+ higher than the opposing die instead of double.

    Rank 1

    • Add "On Break: gain 2 EP."
    • Add Flow 1.

    Rank 2

    • Cause a Break if a die is 3+ higher instead.
    • Increase :fontawesome-burst: DMG by 2.

    Rank 3

    • Decrease EP to 0.
    • Add "On Win: Negate the next X dice in the Clash, where X is your Momentum."
    PAS One Shot

    If you used only one Class or Subclass Move on your last Allegiance turn, increase the PWR of your next Weapon Move by 1.

    Rank 1

    • This effect also applies if you haven't used a Class or Subclass Move this Allegiance turn.
    • Also add Vicious to the Weapon Move.

    Rank 2

    • You may increase PWR by 3 instead if the Weapon Move is the first Action you take on your Allegiance turn. If you do, you may not use any Class or Subclass Moves this turn.
    • If that Weapon Move causes a Break, deal 3 True Harm to the target.

    Rank 3

    • If that Weapon Move causes a Break, gain 1 additional Momentum.
    • Also increase the DMG of that Move by 50%.

    Verdict Hustle Moves

    UTL Heavy Presence

    Cost: 1 FP

    When making a check that involves inspiring someone, coercing someone, or keeping your cool, you may activate this Move to gain automatic Beats equal to 1/2 your Anomaly Tier, to a minimum of 1.

    TRT Threat Detection

    If you spend one minute examining a person, you may ask the GM one of the following questions:

    • Are they armed, even if it's concealed?
    • What's their Anomaly Tier?
    • What is their Armor Tier?
